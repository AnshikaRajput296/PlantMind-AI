INSPECTION REPORT
Equipment: V-201 (Pressure Vessel)
Plant: Plant B, Unit 1
Manufacturer: Sulzer
Inspector: Sunita Rao
Date: May 11, 2025

Findings:
External corrosion observed across approximately 12% of the vessel shell
surface area, primarily on the north-facing weld seam. Wall thickness
measurements indicate corrosion rate consistent with prior inspection
(February 10, 2025). Operating pressure 220 PSI, design pressure 300 PSI.
Hazard classification: corrosive service, flammable contents.

Compliance Reference: ISO 9001, OISD-118, Factory Act 1948 Section 7.
A patch repair was applied; however, full re-coating is recommended within
the next maintenance window to remain within PESO safety margins.

Regulatory gap identified: no documented ISO 45001 risk assessment on file
for this vessel's confined-space entry procedure.
