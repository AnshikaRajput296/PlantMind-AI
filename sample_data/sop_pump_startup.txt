STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE - CENTRIFUGAL PUMP STARTUP
Document: SOP-PUMP-014
Applicable Equipment: P-101, P-102
Plant: Plant A

1. Confirm suction valve fully open before startup.
2. Verify lubrication levels per manufacturer (Grundfos) specification.
3. Check for abnormal vibration or noise during first 60 seconds of operation.
4. Monitor discharge pressure; normal operating range 120-160 PSI.
5. If vibration exceeds 4.5 mm/s RMS, shut down immediately and notify
   the on-shift engineer.
6. Hazards: rotating equipment, potential for seal leak under high pressure.
7. Reference ISO 45001 for personal protective equipment requirements.

Approved by: Amit Verma, Engineering Manager
