MAINTENANCE REPORT
Equipment: P-101 (Centrifugal Pump)
Plant: Plant A, Unit 2
Manufacturer: Grundfos
Serial No: SN-88213A
Date: June 14, 2025
Technician: Rahul Sharma
Approved by: Sunita Rao

Description:
Pump P-101 was taken offline after repeated vibration alarms. Operating pressure
recorded at 145 PSI, discharge temperature 78 C. Inspection revealed bearing
failure with visible pitting on the inner race, consistent with the March and
April 2025 events. Root cause suspected to be shaft misalignment introduced
during the February coupling replacement, compounded by inadequate lubrication
interval. Vibration levels measured at 8.2 mm/s RMS, exceeding ISO 10816
acceptable limits.

Hazards noted: none beyond standard rotating equipment precautions.

Recommended Actions:
1. Perform laser alignment check on P-101 coupling.
2. Revise lubrication schedule from 90-day to 45-day interval.
3. Install continuous vibration monitoring sensor per Honeywell Forge guidelines.
4. Reference OISD-137 for rotating equipment maintenance standards compliance.

This is the third bearing-related failure on P-101 in 2025, indicating a
systemic root cause rather than isolated component defect.
